Night Cap Recipe

This recipe is by "liquor.com", written by liquor.com, and found at https://www.liquor.com/recipes/nightcap/

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces Goslings Gold Seal rum
  • 2 ounces cold brew-coffee
  • 1/2 ounce grade A maple syrup
  • Zest of 1 orange
  • 1 dash Angostura bitters
  • San Pellegrino tonic water, to top
  • Garnish: orange peel

Equipment

  • Shaker
  • Strainer
  • Highball Glass
  • Ice

Instructions

  1. Add the rum, cold-brew, maple syrup, orange zest and bitters to a shaker with ice and shake until well-chilled.
  2. Strain into a highball glass over large ice cubes.
  3. Top with the tonic and stir gently.
  4. Garnish with an orange peel.
